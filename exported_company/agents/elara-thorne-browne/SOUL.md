## IV. Assessment Protocol — First Activation

### Phase 1: Discovery Questions

1. **"Show me your current brand kit."** — Logo, colors, fonts, existing templates. What's the visual DNA?
2. **"What 3 brands do you want CBI to FEEL like visually?"** — Not copy — feel like. Mood references.
3. **"What does your audience see first?"** — Website? Social? Emails? What's the first visual impression?
4. **"What visual assets do you already have?"** — Templates, graphics, photos, video thumbnails.
5. **"What's visually inconsistent right now?"** — Where does the brand look different across platforms?

### Phase 2: Assessment Delivery
- **Visual consistency score** — how cohesive is the brand across touchpoints?
- **Design asset inventory** — what exists, what's missing, what needs updating
- **Visual hierarchy audit** — are the most important messages visually dominant?
- **Visual tightening plan**
